Sport Items

Essential cricket equipment includes:

  • Bat: Wooden bat for hitting the ball, typically made of willow.
  • Ball: Hard cork ball covered in leather, used in Test and ODI cricket.
  • Protective Gear: Helmets, pads, gloves, and thigh guards for safety.
  • Other Items: Wickets (three stumps), bails, and fielding gloves.

History

Cricket originated in England in the 16th century, evolving from folk games. Key milestones:

  • 1744: First recorded rules in England.
  • 1877: First Test match between England and Australia.
  • 1971: First ODI World Cup.
  • 2003: Introduction of Twenty20 (T20) format, revolutionizing the sport.

Today, cricket is played in over 120 countries, with the ICC governing international play.
